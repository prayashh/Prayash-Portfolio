Increase of 70% in booking enquiries after redesigning a travel website into a high-converting lead funnel.

My Role

• UX Designer — Used insights from Google Ads campaigns and Google Ads Analytics to identify user drop-off points and redesign the website’s user experience, information architecture, and inquiry flow.
• Performance Marketer & Developer — Managed Google Ads campaigns, developed the website, and deployed the final product.

Tool Used

Project Duration — 2 Weeks

Problem Statement

Users struggle to discover tour packages and trust the travel website due to cluttered layouts, unclear information hierarchy, and poor conversion-focused design. As a result, users leave without making inquiries, forcing the agency to rely heavily on paid advertisements and increasing CAC(Customer Acquisition Cost).

Objective

  • Improve package discovery and user trust through a clear, intuitive, and conversion-focused user experience.
  • Increase booking inquiries while reducing CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost).

Project Origin

Last year, in April 2025, a Sikkim-based travel agency, Guide Sikkim Tours and Travels, approached us with a challenge — build a website that could turn their business into a strong online presence.

We took the project thinking it would be easy money — after all, for computer science students like us, building websites felt like the simplest thing to do. Within just a few weeks, we delivered the website exactly the way they had asked for it.

But in September 2025, the client came back with a concern — the website wasn’t bringing in any real traffic. It had become nothing more than a piece of social proof. They wanted to turn it into something bigger: a proper lead-generation funnel. So they asked us a new question — what if we run Google Ads for them and used the website to bring in quality travel leads?

Compared to building the website, running ads felt even easier to us — so without thinking twice, we accepted the offer.

First Google Ads Insight

  • Major traffic came from West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, Assam, Bihar, and other Eastern states.
  • West Bengal contributed nearly 60% of total traffic and inquiries.
  • Helped us optimize the experience for the target regional audience.

From the campaign insights, inquiry patterns, and discussions with the client, we identified that most users from the target demographics were looking for honeymoon packages, budget-friendly trips, and family tours. Based on these findings, we created dedicated travel packages tailored to each user segment to improve relevance and conversion.

Here, we discovered that more than 99% of users were browsing through mobile devices. This highlighted the need to design a highly responsive mobile experience while maintaining clear package discoverability and easy navigation.
